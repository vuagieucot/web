Diversed Ecosystem

Amazon Rainforest possesses at least 40,000 kind of plants, 427 kind of mammals, 1,300 kind of birds, 378 kind of reptiles, 400 kind of amphibians and around 3,000 freshwater fishes, including the piranha.

The numbers are so big that visitors will never be bored at Amazy Stay resort. Staying here, you will have opportunity to explore everything about the nature and the lives of animals in Amazon Rainforest.
