Diversity

Amazy Stay serves a variety of food from cultures all around the world, especially northwestern Brazil, Colombia, Peru and other South American countries.

We have 4 main restaurants, representing continents which are Asia, North America, South America, Europe, Australia. Visitors can choose from their preferences of food to enjoy at Amazy Stay.

Quality

At Amazy Stay, we promise to deliver customers the best quality of food. Our ingredients are always fresh and are put through strict selections by experts.

Our chefs are all masterchief from different cultures with strong cooking skills and high comprehension of different cuisines. We want to make sure that our customers can have the best experience at Amazy Stay.
