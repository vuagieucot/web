Explore

Amazy has a wide range of activities and places for visitors to explore

Amazy has a wide range of activities and places for visitors to explore. With approximately 5.5 million km2 of area, we provide numerous activities at different locations so that you can get to every corner of Amazon Rainforest.

The activities are occasionally changed to keep the innovativity of Amazy. Some of our most popular activities:

  • Submarine Travel
  • River Rowing
  • Helicopter Sightseeing

Come to Amazy, visitors will have change to enrich their knowledge about different lives in Amazon Rainforest while enjoying the best vacation ever.


Submarine Travel

Discover the underwater life of Amazon Rainforest

The environment under Amazon river is diversed and proliferate, with 3,000 species living underwater. It is worth paying a trip under Amazon river by our private submarine.

With our submarine, you can travel around Amazon river, explore many new species that you might never met. Especially, sisitors will also have opportunity to be captain of the ship.

You will be guided by our professional captain and be ready to take control of the submarine in a short amount of time. The adventure is yours to decide. This is an exclusive chance giving to our Amazy visitors, available through booking on phone and email.


To get more information about the activity and booking, please contact us here.


River Rowing

Exploring the Amazon Rainforest on the boat

Boats are available for borrowing for visitors who want to explore Amazon by the path of the Amazon river. You can have set the boat floating on river while having snack and enjoying stunning sights.

The trip can also be adventurous sometimes. There are waterfall slopes which are ideal for people who love speed and extreme experience. We recommend only people who are confident about their rowing skills should take this route as there are other safer routes for customer to take too.


To get more information about the activity and booking, please contact us here.


Helicopter Sightseeing

Looking at Amazon from up above

You can get on helicopters and be driven to all around Amazon. You can see everything from above. Helicopter trip is promised to be exciting and worth your spending.
