Sailing Race

Fun and Adventurous

In this activity, you will be controlling our special boats,

CROCODILES!

Our crocodiles are well trained that will never bite people. However, you will need to have some skills to be able to ride it.

After learning how to ride crocodile from our expert trainer, you are eligible to participate in the crocodile race. Your opponents are other visitors of Amazy Stay. You will be racing with others, to get the items floating on the Amazon river and be the fastest one reaching the destination.

Items and questions are put everywhere on Amazon's river. This competition will help visitors explore more about the mighty Amazon's river. Every one joining will get a prize, winner will get the biggest prize from us!

Competitions are not available for register online. Please contact us for more detail.
